This app will help you increase your competence up to someone's solving level by letting you track of their and your solves. You can add up to 20 friends. This app will then show you all the contest name you and your friends solved problems from. Also the number of problems you have solved in a contest that none of your friends did and the number of problems your friends solved that you didn't for each of the problems. You can also check their solved problem for each of your friends by clicking on the contest name. You will get the instruction for using the app below


Register


To regirter you must ensure the information you give is correct. Because once you submit, none of the information can be changed. You will need information mentioned below to register.

  1. Your Name (Though Your profile will have the name you have given in your CodeForces account.)
  2. Valid E-mail Address
  3. Password



To log In use the email address you registered with. Also enter your password correctly. As there is no option for resetting your password you must remember your password.

Profile


Your Profile will have the data you used or achieved in your CodeForces account.



When you go to the Friends page you will be able to see a list of friends you have added so far, also an input cell, an add icon and a remove icon. You can input a valid CF handle (Case Sensitive) and then click the add icon to add a friend which will take a few seconds or you can click the remove icon to remove a friend.

Chase By Contest


When You go to the chase by contest page. You will see a list of contest. You will also see the columns named Lagging and Advancing. In Lagging column you will find the number of problems you are lagging from your friends. In other words, the number of problems your friends solved but you didn't in that contest. Similarly Advancing shows the number of problems you solved but your friends didn't.



Finally, When you click on a contest from the contest list. It will take you to another page where you will get a list of your friends along with the problem IDs they have solved from that specific contest. So that you can know which problem they have solved extra or you have solved extra.
